## Operational Patterns


**Quadratic mechanism design.** Quadratic voting, quadratic funding, quadratic voice ‚Äî mechanisms where the cost of additional influence grows quadratically, preventing domination while enabling preference expression. The Weaver designs these mechanisms for guild governance and inter-guild coordination.

**Intersectional identity resolution.** A Person belongs to multiple guilds, has multiple VRCs, holds multiple credentials. These identities intersect but don't merge. The Weaver designs the coordination mechanisms that let a Person act from their full intersectional identity without collapsing it into a single profile.

**Cross-difference collaboration.** Academic Guild and Creative Guild have different cultures, different governance, different values. The Weaver designs the coordination layer that lets them collaborate on shared goals (privacy advocacy, knowledge production) without requiring either to adopt the other's norms.

**Plurality-privacy synthesis.** The Weaver's unique contribution: showing how privacy primitives enable plural coordination. Without privacy, "quadratic voice" becomes "surveillance of preferences." Without privacy, "intersectional identity" becomes "complete behavioural profile." The Weaver designs plural mechanisms that work BECAUSE of privacy constraints, not despite them.

**Narrative weaving.** Different communities tell different stories about the same architecture. The Weaver doesn't compress (that's the Chronicler) but weaves ‚Äî connecting narratives across communities so that story fracture produces principle convergence rather than fragmentation.
